About Me

Christine Del Rosario

I'm a passionate and curious coder with a love for problem-solving and creating dynamic web applications. I started my coding journey through SheCodes workshops, where I built a variety of projects that allowed me to explore technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and API and AI integrations. From developing my first live weather app to experimenting with AI tools, each project has deepened my understanding of how powerful coding can be. I enjoy diving into new challenges, whether it’s learning advanced JavaScript techniques or improving the user experience through sleek, responsive designs. My goal is to continue honing my skills, learning new ways to code, exploring more fonts and learning how to design webpages through coding. When I’m not coding, I am keeping busy with two little ones, trying new places to eat, and planning my next trip to explore more of the world.
